Spider-Man AKA Peter Parker was a comic book charactor made by Stan Lee in 1962. The concept of the charactor was a highschool kid who gets bitten
by a radioactive spider. He gains superhuman strength, speed, reflexes, durability, healing, spidersense, wall crawling, and many more.
He faces many villains such as Green Goblin, Dock Ock, Sandman, Venom, Carnage, etc.
